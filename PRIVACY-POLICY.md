# Privacy Policy - Bookmark Duplicate Cleaner

## Data Collection
This extension:
- Only accesses your bookmarks to find duplicates
- Stores basic usage statistics locally on your device
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not collect personal information

## Data Storage
- All bookmark data remains on your computer
- Usage statistics (scan count, duplicate count) are stored locally
- No data is transmitted over the network

## Permissions Used
- `bookmarks`: To read and manage your bookmarks
- `storage`: To save settings and statistics locally
- `alarms`: For maintenance tasks

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via GitHub issues.
